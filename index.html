History

Alto Music started in 2015 when Justin Aquino first made music with a recording kit she received as a birthday present. Now she produces music for big artists from all over the world, including BTS, Twice, and Beyonce.

Mission

To secure the means, spaces, and resources needed for the development of tomorrow's artist and their original expression.

Vision

We see a world where the artist is supported and enabled to pursue the creation of amazing music, and to organically find connection with their audience.
