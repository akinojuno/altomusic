Services


Live Streaming

PACKAGE 1: We can stream your graduation or event from your location on YouTube or Facebook in multi-camera HD video and audio. You can record a voice-over for the entire program and also supply photos, video clips, program notes, etc. that we would weave into the live production. Students and faculty can participate via Zoom or Skype and the broadcast can be publicly accessible or private.


PACKAGE 2: PACKAGE 1 without the live elements. Prior to the event date, our crew can visit your location to film exterior or interior shots and record messages from the principal and selected faculty. From this content and any program voice-over, photos, video clips, program notes, etc that you supply, we would create a video for your review and work with you to finalize the production. We would then stream the video live at the appropriate date and time.
